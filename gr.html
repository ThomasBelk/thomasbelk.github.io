Galactic Resurgence

Galactic Resugence is a first person platformer and shooter in early development. It is currently being devloped as a group for the Game Programing in Unity. The final game is expected to feature 3 levels and a final boss level, 2d Galactica style space ship shooting to get between planets (levels), and a variety of power-ups.
