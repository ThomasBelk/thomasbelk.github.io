Just Keep Flying

Just Keep Flying is a first person multiplayer party game about keeping your airship in the sky. It is currently being developed in Unreal Engine 5 as a project for Game Concept Design and has a projected release date of April 15th 2024! The game's will blend steampunk and magical elements and hopefully be a fun game for you to play with your friends. More updates coming soon.
