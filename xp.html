XP Dimension Mod

XP Dimension Mod is a work-in-progress personal project. The idea revolves around expanding gameplay around experience. The goal is to have a whole new dimension, new mobs, a new villager type, new ores, and a new custom enchantment system. So far I've implemented experience ore (an ore that drops a raw experience item), edible experience (the player can eat raw experience and it will give them experience), an air block (which blows the player up in the air), and a new item that propells the player when using an elytra. I also have been experimenting with custom dimension and biome generation, but nothing noteable has come of it yet. The goal would be able to get the mod out by the end of the year, but depending on my workload that may or may not be achievable.
